How to Play Bubble Rush

Objective:

Pop the correct bubble indicated by the hit number within the time limit to earn points.

Controls:

Use your mouse or touchpad to click on the bubbles.

Gameplay:

  • Each bubble will display a number.
  • Click on the bubble that matches the displayed number.
  • Earn points for each correct hit.
  • Avoid penalties for hitting the wrong bubble or running out of time.

Scoring:

Earn 10 points for each correct hit. Lose 10 points for hitting the wrong bubble or running out of time.

Time Limit:

The game lasts for 60 seconds. Try to earn as many points as possible within this time frame.

Difficulty:

Bubbles may move faster as the game progresses.

Game Over:

The game ends when the timer runs out.

Rules

  1. Hit the Right Bubble: Click on the bubble that matches the displayed number. Earn points for hitting the correct bubble. Lose points for hitting the wrong bubble or running out of time.
  2. Score Points: Earn 10 points for each correct hit.
  3. Avoid Penalties: Lose 10 points for hitting the wrong bubble or running out of time.
  4. Time Limit: The game lasts for 60 seconds. Try to score as many points as possible within this time frame.
  5. Beat the Clock: Pop bubbles quickly to maximize your score before time runs out.
  6. Achieve High Scores: Aim to beat your own record and achieve the highest score possible.
  7. Game Over: The game ends when the timer runs out. Your final score is displayed, and you can choose to play again to beat your high score.
